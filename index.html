Los Angeles is full of interesting and exciting attractions, but the more common places impacted my life the most

I was born in the Topanga hospital in Los Angeles, California on June 7, 2002. I live in Van Nuys, which is the largest city in LA county with respect to population and poverty rate. I went to school at The Buckley School in Sherman Oaks for grades seven through twelve.

One of my favorite spots in LA is the Galleria, the movie theatre mall. This is where I skipped school with my friends to go see Avengers: Endgame the day it came out...

Maybe my favorite place in all of Los Angeles is Sherman Oaks Little League, where I played baseball from ages 4-12. It is where I discovered my love for baseball and made friends that are still some of the best people I know. I had an incredible run there, as five banners with my name on it still hang in the rafters. Here's what I won:

  • 9-10 State Champions (All-Stars)
  • 10-11 Sub-Division Runner-up (Semi-Finals)
  • 11-12 Sub-Division Runner-up
  • 12u Tournament Champions (Clemson Tigers)
  • 10u Tournament Champions (Yankees)
